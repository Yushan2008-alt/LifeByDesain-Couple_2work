# Product Brief: LifebyDesign Couple

## 1. Product Vision

Digital journaling system untuk couple yang mau invest intentionally di hubungan mereka. Bukan aplikasi krisis atau therapy replacement, tapi tool struktural yang bantu pasangan understand each other lebih dalam, align di values dan goals, dan celebrate growth bareng.

Positioning: **intentional relationship maintenance**, bukan relationship rescue.

## 2. Core Philosophy

Produk ini dibangun atas 4 prinsip dasar:

**Private journal, shared insights.** Jurnal tetap private sampai user mau share. Data tracking (mood, behavior, score) shared supaya ada transparency, tapi isi pikiran tetap kamu yang kontrol.

**Cooling off via AI translation.** Emosi mentah ga langsung dilempar ke pasangan. AI bantu refine ke bahasa yang lebih clear dan non-accusatory, user review dulu, baru share. Ini delay mechanic yang sehat buat relationship.

**Celebration over fixing.** Produk ga cuma diagnostic ("apa yang salah"), tapi juga recognition ("apa yang udah bagus"). Wins, milestones, streak semua visible.

**Positive framing.** Bahasa produk, prompt, dan feedback semua ngarah ke growth mindset, bukan problem-focused.

## 3. Core Concept: 3-Layer Journaling

Produk terdiri dari 3 layer temporal yang saling nyambung:

### Layer 1: Daily (low effort, high frequency)
Data mentah. Gampang, cepat, habit-forming.

### Layer 2: Weekly (medium effort, medium frequency)
Reflection dan discussion. Transformasi data jadi percakapan.

### Layer 3: Long-term (high effort, low frequency)
Direction. Goals dan big picture.

Daily feed Weekly. Weekly feed Long-term. Tiga layer ini harus terasa nyambung, bukan 3 aplikasi terpisah yang kebetulan digabung.

## 4. Target User

Couple yang:
- Udah dalam relationship stabil (bukan krisis acute)
- Mau invest di hubungan mereka dengan intentional effort
- Nyaman dengan journaling atau self-reflection
- Siap commit berdua (kedua partner harus aktif)
- Relationship status: dating serius, tunangan, menikah, long distance (inklusif)

Bukan untuk:
- Couple in crisis yang butuh therapy
- Casual dating tanpa commitment
- Solo user (ini strictly couple product)

## 5. Feature Breakdown

### 5.1 Daily Layer

**Mood Tracking**
- Quick-tap mood selector (emoji + intensity)
- Optional tag: contextual keywords (work, family, health, intimacy, etc)
- Visible ke partner sebagai pattern (bukan real-time notification)

**Behavior / Productivity Tracking**
- Customizable daily habits per user (contoh: workout, read, meditate, date night, tidur cukup)
- Check-off harian
- Shared visibility: partner bisa liat tapi ga ada judgment UI

**Private Journaling**
- Free-form text entry
- Tagged by date otomatis
- Private by default, ga share kecuali user explicit mau

**Emotion Dump**
- Space khusus untuk ngeluarin emosi mentah tentang hubungan atau hal related
- Private selama week
- End of week: AI refine, user review, share ke partner (optional)

**Shared To-do List**
- Real-time sync antara 2 user
- Add, check off, delete by either partner
- Categorize: home, errands, social, plans, finances
- Light utility yang bikin app open daily

**Shared Streak**
- Team-based counter: berapa hari berturut-turut kedua partner engage
- Break kalau salah satu ga engage 2 hari berturut-turut (buffer 1 hari)
- Visible di home screen sebagai motivator
- Bukan competitive, tapi collaborative

### 5.2 Weekly Layer

**Weekly Check-in**
- Scheduled ritual (default: Sunday evening, customizable)
- Duration estimasi: 20-30 menit
- Default flow: synchronous (dikerjakan bareng), tapi bisa async (masing-masing isi dulu, baca hasil bareng)
- Structured flow dengan prompts

**AI Emotion Translation**
- Core moat of the product
- Raw emotion dump dari week sebelumnya → AI refine
- Full replacement (bahasa lebih clear, tone non-accusatory), raw version tetap visible ke user
- Review-before-share mechanic: user bisa edit atau cancel sebelum lempar ke partner
- Target tone: honest but kind, specific but not blaming

**360 Scoring System**
- 5 dimensi hubungan (tentative): Communication, Intimacy, Support, Fun, Effort
- Each user score 2x:
  - Self-perception (gue ngerasa kita di dimensi X berapa)
  - Perceived partner (gue pikir pasangan gue ngerasa kita di dimensi X berapa)
- Gap analysis: perbedaan antara self-score vs partner's actual score = insight valuable
- Visual: radar chart atau bar comparison

**Review Last Week's Checklist**
- Dari weekly check-in sebelumnya, ada action items yang di-commit
- Week ini: review, mana yang done, yang belum, kenapa
- Bukan grading, tapi akuntabilitas gentle

**Wins Celebration**
- Section khusus untuk acknowledge pencapaian week ini
- Bisa relationship win (quality time bagus, komunikasi lebih lancar) atau individual win yang di-share
- Visible badge / marker di history

**Template Library Access**
- Library of pre-written prompts untuk edge case scenarios
- Positive Relationship Toolkit
- Passive: user akses sendiri kalau butuh, ga pop-up otomatis
- Contoh template: "Conflict Recovery Conversation", "Planning Our Next Quarter", "Handling Disagreement on Finances"

**Self-Suggestion**
- Based on user's own pattern (mood, behavior tracking week itu)
- AI-generated, ditujukan ke diri sendiri (bukan saran tentang pasangan)
- Contoh: "Minggu ini mood kamu low di hari Rabu-Kamis. Pattern ini muncul 3 minggu berturut-turut. Mungkin worth check schedule mid-week kamu?"

### 5.3 Monthly Layer

**Monthly Recap**
- Auto-generated end of month
- Visual trend: mood, behavior, score over 4 weeks
- Highlight: streak terbaik, peak mood week, improvement areas
- Share-able ke partner (both liat hasil yang sama)

**Milestone Celebration**
- Badge/marker untuk achievements:
  - Monthly streak achieved
  - First 360 score gap closed
  - Anniversary dalam app
  - Goal completion
- Visual satisfying, bikin momen rasa "we did this together"

### 5.4 6-Month / Yearly Layer

**Joint Goals**
- Set di awal periode (6 bulan atau 1 tahun)
- Kategori bisa beda per partner (contoh: partner A: "travel lebih", partner B: "komunikasi lebih terbuka")
- Tapi visible ke kedua belah pihak
- Progress tracking: incremental check-in tiap bulan

**Big Recap (Wrapped-style)**
- End of period (6 bulan / 1 tahun)
- Visual storytelling: key stats, growth, memorable moments
- Share-able ke social media (privacy-controlled, generic visual tanpa data sensitive)
- Emotional peak moment yang bikin orang excited buat lanjut period berikutnya

**Relationship Report Card**
- Comprehensive view: 360 score trend over period, goals achieved, patterns learned
- Narrative summary dari AI
- Foundation buat set goals period berikutnya

## 6. Supporting Features

### Longitudinal Memory View
- Timeline view dari entries & scores over time
- Bisa filter by theme, date range, mood
- "Pernah ingat hari kita argue soal X? Ini catatan mood kita saat itu..."
- Value compounds over time, reason user stay subscribed beyond Year 1

### Privacy Controls
- Explicit control per entry type:
  - Daily journal: default private
  - Mood tag: default shared (no content detail)
  - Emotion dump: private until weekly share (post AI translation)
  - Goals: shared
- Data export anytime (JSON/PDF)
- Data not used for AI training
- Break-up scenario: reset pair connection, data masing-masing bisa di-export atau dihapus

### Partner Activity Awareness
- Gentle visibility: partner udah isi mood hari ini / belum
- Bukan invasive notification, cuma subtle indicator
- Push notification opt-in buat reminder (web push / email)

## 7. User Journey

### First-time Onboarding
- Partner A sign up, dapat unique token
- Partner A invite Partner B via link/code
- Partner B sign up dengan token, connected
- Joint intro flow:
  - Relationship context (how long together, current life stage)
  - Initial values & priorities (light, not deep yet)
  - Set first week's focus
- Optional: initial 360 score sebagai baseline

### Daily Usage (2-5 menit)
Typical user flow:
1. Open app
2. Check shared to-do (add/complete)
3. Tap mood
4. Check-off habits
5. Optional: write emotion dump if something happened
6. Glance partner status (did they engage today?)

### Weekly Ritual (20-30 menit)
Recommended flow both together:
1. Open weekly check-in
2. Review week's data (mood, behavior pattern, streak)
3. Share AI-translated emotion dumps (each partner)
4. Do 360 scoring (both fill, see results)
5. Discuss gaps, wins, last week's commitments
6. Set focus for next week
7. Close ritual dengan acknowledgment

### Monthly Moment (15 menit)
- Auto-open when month ends
- Review together atau masing-masing
- Short reflection, minimal effort

### Long-term Recap (30-60 menit)
- End of 6 bulan / 1 tahun
- Set aside time, special moment
- Review big recap
- Reset goals & commitments for next period

## 8. Data Model (Conceptual)

**Per couple:**
- Connection record (pair of 2 user accounts linked via token)
- Start date, current period (6-month / yearly)
- Joint goals list
- Shared to-do items
- Streak counter
- Weekly check-in records
- Monthly recap snapshots
- Period recap snapshots

**Per user (within couple):**
- Profile (name, optional avatar)
- Daily entries:
  - Mood log (datetime, value, tags)
  - Behavior log (habit check-offs)
  - Journal entries (private)
  - Emotion dump entries (private raw + AI-refined version)
- Individual goal progress
- Streak participation
- 360 scores (self + perceived partner)

## 9. Platform & Technical Scope

**MVP:**
- Web app, responsive design (mobile-first)
- PWA installable (home screen icon, offline minimal caching)
- Email notification (primary channel)
- Browser push notification (opt-in, secondary)
- Modern browser support (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, mobile browsers)

**Languages:**
- Bahasa Indonesia (primary)
- English (optional, V2)

**Third-party dependencies:**
- Authentication: Supabase Auth
- Database: Supabase PostgreSQL
- File storage: Supabase Storage (untuk avatar, minimal)
- AI: external LLM API (TBD per cost consideration)
- Email: Resend atau similar
- Payment: Midtrans / Xendit (Indonesian payment gateway)

## 10. Content Requirements

Di luar technical build, produk butuh content library:

**Template Library (Positive Relationship Toolkit)**
- 30-50 template prompts untuk variasi scenarios
- Kategori: conflict, planning, intimacy, communication, life changes, celebration
- Ditulis dalam bahasa yang warm, non-clinical

**Weekly Check-in Prompts Bank**
- Rotating prompts biar ga monotonous
- Variasi depth: surface (week recap) sampai deep (values discussion)

**AI Emotion Translation Prompt Engineering**
- System prompt yang consistent: tone guidelines, do's & don'ts
- Training examples untuk edge cases
- Testing matrix sebelum launch

**Monthly & Yearly Recap Copy**
- Template narrative untuk auto-generated recap
- Variasi biar ga generic

## 11. Out of Scope (V1)

Feature yang valid tapi sengaja ga dimasukin di MVP untuk keep scope manageable:

- Native iOS / Android app (V2 consideration)
- Video call integration (therapy referral)
- AI chatbot conversational (rule-based prompts only di V1)
- Voice journaling (text only)
- Group feature (couple + therapist view)
- Social sharing beyond yearly recap
- Gamification beyond streak (no points, levels, leaderboards)
- Multi-language beyond Indonesian
- Integration dengan calendar apps
- Integration dengan wearable (mood from biometric)
- Guided meditation / audio content

## 12. Success Criteria (Product-wise)

Produk dianggap sukses (dari product perspective, bukan business) kalau:

**Engagement metrics:**
- Daily active rate per couple: >50% (artinya setidaknya 1 partner aktif per hari)
- Weekly check-in completion rate: >60% of active couples
- Streak average: >14 days per couple

**Value delivery metrics:**
- 360 score gap improvement over 3 months: average 15%+ reduction
- User-reported relationship improvement survey: >70% agree
- Feature usage distribution: semua layer (daily/weekly/monthly) actively used, bukan cuma daily

**Retention metrics:**
- 6-month retention: >50%
- Yearly retention: >40%
- Renewal rate: >60%
